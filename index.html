OUR STAND

EKETEKE-OHA (COMMON WEALTH) stands to affirm, that, mankind is gifted with the power to discern between what is Good or Bad, Light or Dark, Right or wrong, safe or unsafe -and True or False! So, before you twist one against another for selfish interest, first,- LET, YOUR CONCIENCE JUDGE YOU!!

WHO WE ARE

EKETEKE OHA Council Council is registered as an Incorporate Trustee by Government in July, 2025 with registration identity CAC/IT No. 8615089.

AIMS

  • To protect the overall interests of the citizens of ALFRED STATES world-wide from any form of critical Incident/Accident Emergency.

OBJECTIVES

  • To establish a platform for collective decisions on all issues that borders on economic development, wealth creation and safety of our cherished value!

OUR PILOT PROJECTS

In line with our eleemosynary, we hereby establish the following projects which we intend to achieve between 2025-2030 EKETEKE-OHA calendars.

ALLIANCE FOR REGIONAL ECONOMY DEVELOPMENT (ALFRED)

The cardinal objectives are but not limited to repatriating all the businesses belonging to the citizens of ALFRED STATES back to our home states.

PILOT TWO

CONFERENCE OF ALFRED STATES (CASE)

Globally, we have learned in a hard way, that, the apparent low level of awareness among the locals on topical leadership issues is the major culprit in their vulnerability to crises and other misdemeanours.

For this, (just to help stem the miasma of ugly incidents before, during and after important local activities), we shall:

  • Advocate for streamlined process of engagements and dialogues among the locals to ensure peaceful and orderly behaviours during such occasions.
