GENESIS

EKETEKE-OHA (COMMON WEALTH) stands from generation onto to generation, to strengthen our cherished value, driven with extremism, as handed-down to us, by our Patriarchs Honourable OZIOKO OHA, the father of Eketeke, the father of seven cities inhabited by man and woman who share common interests in the affairs of one another to this day. The common value they share are encompassing benevolence, selfless service to humanity and undisputed leadership-OZIOKO-EJI Ancient Title, which remains in the families to this day. Without contesting that with any living soul, we, the family of Ekektke-Oha hereby affirm, to take the inheritance to another level, by sharing our thoughts and values, with all the members of our communities, anywhere they are, - to live together safely and defend our cherished values, until we join those who came before us.

Our Policy is simple

“To live life in self-esteem, safety and contentment, with or at best above others, while maintaining what we cherish, to the point of dying for it or coercing those who do not share our thoughts and believes. We keep this as our flambeau, from generation to generation.

JAAMIKE ZELUWA MBADIWE

WHO WE ARE

EKETEKE OHA Council is registered as an Incorporate Trustee by Government to promote the ideal of selfless services to humanity by using organized platforms to provide safety awareness,selfhelp and disaster emergency assistance.

AIMS

  • To foster ideal of voluntary services among people both in the urban and rural areas, where the activities of some regulatory agencies of government are grossly inadequate.
  • To complement the relevant statutory agencies on all issues bordering on industrial, home, fire, public safety and voluntary emergency assistance.
  • To provide FREE short duration training and awareness courses on safety, fire and emergency rescue operations for business operators, market women, youths, town unions, schools and recreation centres.

OBJECTIVES

  • To establish a culture of collective responsibility on all issues that promote incident and accident prevention, management and control and voluntary services among men as cherished culture and trending value.
  • To provide advanced awareness training, warning and advice on any topical national and global issues where the conducts of the people could impact either negatively or positively on their lives and/or the cherished reputation of the community.
  • Finally, we are not interested in politics, however, we shall continually make our positions clear on specific issues of contemporary relevance to man, his thoughts and values with emphases on food-on-the table, safety and friendships.

OUR PILOT PROJECTS

In line with our eleemosynary, we hereby establish the following projects which we intend to achieve between 2025-2030 EKETEKE-OHA calendars.
We members of the Trustees believe that, the projects will impact positively on our community.

ALLIANCE FOR REGIONAL ECONOMY DEVELOPMENT (ALFRED)

The cardinal objectives are but not limited to repatriating all the businesses belonging to the citizens of ALFRED STATES back to our home states.

To attract investments to the ALFRED STATES with full guarantees of free industrial Safety programs of action, fair regulatory compliances, friendly host communities and profits.

PILOT TWO

CONFERENCE OF ALFRED STATES (CASES)

Globally, we have learned in a hard way, that, the apparent low level of awareness among the locals on topical leadership issues is the major culprit in their vulnerability to crises and other misdemeanours.

For this, (just to help stem the miasma of ugly incidents before, during and after important local activities), we shall:

  • Advocate for streamlined process of engagements and dialogues among the locals to ensure peaceful and orderly behaviours during such occasions.
  • Compliment local interest groups and/ or any agency of government in ensuring peace and safety of the king-makers while steering clear from any partisan activities.
  • Assist any agency of government that may seek for our assistance as partners in Safety in the processes of choosing leaders.
  • Choose from the locals those who will support our community-based safety and voluntary emergency assistance projects to become our SAFETY GLOBAL.
